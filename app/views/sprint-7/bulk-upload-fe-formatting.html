Formatting a bulk upload file

The tables below show information about each field in the bulk upload file and how to complete it correctly.

LARS_QAN

Regulated and non regulated qualifications are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference) or QAN (Qualification Accreditation Number) code.

Codes can be found at the Learning Aims Reference Service.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 8
Example 50079281
Z9483628
00280206
5011752X
Notes
 Codes must be 8 digits. For codes with less than 8 digits add one or more zeros to the beginning of the code.

WHO_IS_THIS_COURSE_FOR

Information that will help the learner decide whether this course is suitable for them, the learning experience and opportunities they can expect from the course.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 2000
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

ENTRY_REQUIREMENTS

Specific skills, licences, vocational or academic requirements. For example, DBS, driving licence, computer knowledge, literacy or numeracy requirements.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

WHAT_YOU_WILL_LEARN

The main topics, units or modules of the course a learner can expect, include key features. For example, communication, team leadership and time management.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

HOW_YOU_WILL_LEARN

The methods used to deliver the course. For example, classroom based exercises, a work environment or online study materials.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

WHAT_YOU_WILL_NEED_TO_BRING

What the learner will need to access or bring to the course. For example, personal protective clothing, tools, devices or internet access.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

HOW_YOU_WILL_BE_ASSESSED

The ways a learner will be assessed. For example, workplace assessment, written assignments, exams, group or individual project work or portfolio of evidence.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

WHERE_NEXT

The further opportunities a learner can expect after successfully completing the course. For example, a higher level course, apprenticeship or entry to employment.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 500
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

COURSE_NAME

The course name displayed on the National Career Service, Find a course.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 255
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

ID

A reference that will help you identify this course in your internal systems. This will not be visible to learners.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 255

DELIVERY_MODE

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Classroom based
Online
Work based
Notes
 For courses with more than one delivery mode, each course must be entered on a separate row.

START_DATE

When to complete Courses with a specific start date.
Mandatory Yes
Example 24/09/2022
Notes
 Start dates must be after today's date.

For courses with more than one start date, each course must be entered on a separate row.

If your course does not have a specific start date leave blank.

FLEXIBLE_START_DATE

When to complete Courses with a flexible start date.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
Notes
 If your course does not have a flexible start date leave blank.

VENUE

You can only enter a venue that is already added to Publish to the course directory.

If you have a new venue you must add this to your locations before you can complete a bulk upload.

When to complete Classroom based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 255
Example My Location Name

NATIONAL_DELIVERY

If you can deliver the course anywhere in England you will be shown as a national provider.

When to complete Work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
No

REGION

This field must be kept blank.

SUB_REGION

All the areas where you can deliver the course.

When to complete Work based courses that are not delivered nationally.
Mandatory Yes
Example Derby;Norfolk;Leicester;Brent
What to enter Regions and sub regions

URL

The webpage for this course.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 255
Example https://www.provider.com/coursename

COST

The cost of the course to the learner.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Currency GBP
Example 1500.00

COST_DESCRIPTION

A description of what the cost includes and additional costs to the learner. For example, assessment, exam fees or study materials.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory No
Character limit 255

DURATION

The number of days, weeks, months or years the course runs for.

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 3
Example 18

When to complete Classroom, online and work based courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Days
Weeks
Months
Years

When to complete Classroom based courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Full time
Part time
Flexible
Notes
 For courses with more than one study mode, each course must be entered on a separate row.

When to complete Classroom based courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Daytime

 For courses with more than one attendance pattern, each course must be entered on a separate row.
