Formatting an apprenticeship data upload file

The tables below show information about each field in the apprenticeship data upload file and how to complete it correctly.

STANDARD_CODE

Standards are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference Service) codes.

Codes can be found at Find a learning aim.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes

STANDARD_VERSION

Standards on Publish to the course directory are version 1.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes
What to enter
1

APPRENTICESHIP_INFORMATION

Provide information for employers about how you will deliver this apprenticeship training course. The information should meet style and compliance guidelines.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes
Character limit
750
Notes
Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

APPRENTICESHIP_WEBPAGE

The webpage for this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
No
Example
https://www.provider.com/apprenticeshipname

CONTACT_EMAIL

The email address for this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes
Example
name@example.com

CONTACT_PHONE

The telephone number for this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes
Example
01632 960 001
07700 900 982
+44 0808 157 0192

CONTACT_URL

The contact webpage for your organisation.

When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
No
Example
https://www.provider.com/contact

DELIVERY_METHOD

Apprenticeship training can be delivered by the following methods:

  • classroom - at your location on day or block release
  • employer - at the employer's location
  • both - at your location and the employer's location
When to complete
All apprenticeship training courses
Mandatory
Yes
What to enter
Classroom
Employer
Both

VENUE

You can only enter a venue that is already added to Publish to the course directory.

If you have a new venue you must add this to your locations before you can complete a bulk upload.

When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both
Mandatory
Yes
Character limit
255
Example
My Location Name
Notes
For apprenticeship training courses with more than one venue, each apprenticeship must be entered on a separate row.

RADIUS

How far you are willing to travel from the venue to deliver this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Both and not delivered across the whole of England.
Mandatory
Yes
Unit
Miles
Notes
The radius must be between 1 and 874.

DELIVERY_MODE

How the training is delivered for this apprenticeship training course.

  • day - for example one day a week at the training provider's location
  • block - for example 3-4 weeks at the training provider's location
  • employer - training is delivered at the employer's location
When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both
Mandatory
Yes
What to enter
Day
Block
Employer
Example
Day;Block;Employer

ACROSS_ENGLAND

You can deliver the apprenticeship training course anywhere in England and you will be shown as a national provider for this course.

When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Both
Mandatory
Yes
What to enter
Yes
No
Notes
For an apprenticeship training course not delivered across England you must enter a RADIUS.

NATIONAL_DELIVERY

You can deliver the apprenticeship training course anywhere in England and you will be shown as a national provider for this course.

When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer
Mandatory
Yes
What to enter
Yes
No

REGION

This field must be kept blank.

SUB_REGION

All the areas where you can deliver the course.

When to complete
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer
Mandatory
Yes
Example
Derby;Norfolk;Leicester;Brent
What to enter
Regions and sub regions
