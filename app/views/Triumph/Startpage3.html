Publish to the course directory

This service is for learning providers who are contracted to the Education and Skills Funding Agency and provide courses in England for people aged 16-18 or 19+.

The service allows providers to add and edit courses, apprenticeships and T Levels.

The information entered will be visible to learners searching for opportunities on the National Career Service, Find a course, TLevels.gov.uk and employers searching for training on Find apprenticeship training for your apprentice.

Start now

How to access Publish to the course directory

You will need a DfE Sign-in account to access Publish to the course directory, you can create an account and request access to your organisation.

Once your request has been approved, contact the Course Directory Support Team who will provide access to the service.

Provider primary contact details

Your organisation contact information is updated from The UK Register of Learning Providers (UKRLP) and visible to learners and employers searching for courses, apprenticeships and T Levels.

Your primary contact details should be up to date at all times, missing details may result in information not being diplayed. You can update your primary contact details at any time.
