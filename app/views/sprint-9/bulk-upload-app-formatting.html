Formatting a bulk upload file

The tables below show information about each field in the bulk upload file for apprenticeship training courses and how to complete it correctly.

STANDARD_CODE

Standards are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference) or QAN (Qualification Accreditation Number) code.

Codes can be found at Find a learning aim.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
Notes
 Standards must be approved for delivery.

STANDARD_VERSION

Standards on Publish to the course directory are version 1.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter 1

Frameworks

Frameworks have now been replaced by standards, while we continue to update the service leave the following columns blank:

  • FRAMEWORKS_CODE
  • FRAMEWORKS_PROG_TYPE
  • FRAMEWORKS_PATHWAY_CODE

Read more about the withdrawal of apprenticeship frameworks.

APPRENTICESHIP_INFORMATION

Provide information for employers about how you will deliver this apprenticeship training course. The information should meet style and compliance guidelines.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 750
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

APPRENTICESHIP_WEBPAGE

The webpage for this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory No
Example https://www.provider.com/apprenticeshipname

CONTACT_EMAIL

The contact for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
Example name@example.com

CONTACT_PHONE

The contact for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
Example 01632 960 001

CONTACT_URL

The contact page for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory No
Example https://www.provider.com/contact

DELIVERY_METHOD

Where the apprenticeship training will be delivered.*Add in definition of what to enter*

When to complete All apprenticeship training courses.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Classroom
Employer
Both

VENUE

You can only enter a venue that is already added to Publish to the course directory.

If you have a new venue you must add this to your locations before you can complete a bulk upload.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 255
Example My Location Name
Notes
 For apprenticeship training courses with more than one venue, each apprenticeship must be entered on a separate row.

RADIUS

How far you are willing to travel from the venue to deliver the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Both and not delivered across the whole of England.
Mandatory Yes
Unit
 Miles
Notes
 The radius must be between 1 and 874.

DELIVERY_MODE

*Add in definitions*

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Day
Block
Both
Example Day;Block;Both

ACROSS_ENGLAND

If the apprenticeship training course can be delivered across the whole of England.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Both.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
No

NATIONAL_DELIVERY

If you can deliver the apprenticeship training course anywhere in England you will be shown as a national provider.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
No

REGION

This field must be kept blank.

SUB_REGION

All the areas where you can deliver the course.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer.
Mandatory Yes
Example Derby;Norfolk;Leicester;Brent
What to enter Regions and sub regions
