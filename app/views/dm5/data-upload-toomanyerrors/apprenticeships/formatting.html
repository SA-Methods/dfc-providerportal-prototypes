Apprenticeship file format requirements

The tables below show information about each field in the apprenticeship data upload file and how to complete it correctly.

  1. Standard code
  2. Standard version
  3. Apprenticeship information
  4. Apprenticeship webpage
  5. Contact email
  6. Contact phone
  1. Contact URL
  2. Delivery method
  3. Venue
  4. Your venue reference
  5. Radius
  6. Delivery mode
  1. Across England
  2. National delivery
  3. Sub-region

STANDARD_CODE

Mandatory
Yes
Notes
Standards are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference Service) codes. Codes can be found at Find a learning aim.

STANDARD_VERSION

Mandatory
Yes
Example
1
Notes
Standards on Publish to the course directory are version 1.

APPRENTICESHIP_INFORMATION

Mandatory
Yes
Character limit
750
Notes
Provide information for employers about how you will deliver this apprenticeship training course. The information should meet style and compliance guidelines.

APPRENTICESHIP_WEBPAGE

Mandatory
No
Example
https://www.provider.com/apprenticeshipname
Notes
The webpage for this apprenticeship training course.

CONTACT_EMAIL

Mandatory
Yes
Example
name@example.com
Notes
The email address for this apprenticeship training course.

CONTACT_PHONE

Mandatory
Yes
Example
01632 960 001
07700 900 982
+44 0808 157 0192
Notes
The telephone number for this apprenticeship training course.

CONTACT_URL

Mandatory
No
Example
https://www.provider.com/contact
Notes
The contact webpage for your organisation.

DELIVERY_METHOD

Mandatory
Yes
Example
Classroom
Employer
Both
Notes
Apprenticeship training can be delivered by the following methods:
Classroom - at your location on day or block release
Employer - at the employer's location
Both - at your location and the employer's location

VENUE

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both.
Character limit
255
Example
My location name
Notes
You can only enter a venue that is already added to Publish to the course directory. If you have a new venue you must add this to your locations before you can complete an apprenticeship data upload. For apprenticeship training courses with more than one venue, each apprenticeship must be entered on a separate row.

YOUR_VENUE_REFERENCE

Mandatory
Yes
Chartacter limit
255
Notes
A unique reference that will help you identify this venue in your internal systems.

RADIUS

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Both and not delivered across the whole of England.
Notes
How far you are willing to travel from the venue to deliver this apprenticeship training course. The radius must be between 1 and 874.

DELIVERY_MODE

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Classroom or Both.
Example
Day;Block;Employer
Notes
How the training is delivered for this apprenticeship training course.
Day - for example one day a week at the training provider's location
Block - for example 3-4 weeks at the training provider's location
Employer - training is delivered at the employer's location

ACROSS_ENGLAND

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Both.
Example
Yes/No
Notes
You can deliver the apprenticeship training course anywhere in England and you will be shown as a national provider for this course. For an apprenticeship training course not delivered across England you must enter a RADIUS.

NATIONAL_DELIVERY

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer.
Example
Yes/No
Notes
You can deliver the apprenticeship training course anywhere in England and you will be shown as a national provider for this course.

SUB_REGION

Mandatory
When DELIVERY_METHOD is Employer
Example
Derby;Norfolk;Leicester;Brent
Notes
All the areas where you can deliver the apprenticeship training course.
