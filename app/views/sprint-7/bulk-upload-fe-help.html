Regulated and non regulated qualifications are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference) or QAN (Qualification Accreditation Number) code.

Codes can be found at the Learning Aims Reference Service.

Mandatory Yes
Character limit 8
Format Number, text
Example 50079281 or Z9483628
Notes Reference codes are 8 digits. Regulated qualifications may be 7 digits with an X. Non regulated qualifications codes must start with Z followed by 7 digits.
