Bulk upload

You can upload a CSV file to update your courses in Publish to the course directory, or you can add, edit or delete each course individually.

Uploading and publishing a bulk upload file will replace all of your published courses. To create the file you can:

  • download a blank template and add your course information
  • download and edit a file containing your published course information

Preparing your bulk upload

To ensure your bulk upload is successful you must:

  • add new venues to the service prior to your bulk upload
  • complete all mandatory fields in your bulk upload
  • check and remove duplicate venue entries

Each field in the bulk upload file has a character limit and exceeding this limit will return errors, read more about how to complete a bulk upload file.
