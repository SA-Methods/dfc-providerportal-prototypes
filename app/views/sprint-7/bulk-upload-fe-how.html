Bulk upload

You can upload a CSV file to update your courses in Publish to the course directory.

Creating your bulk upload file

To create the file you can:

  • download your published course information (CSV)
  • download a template (CSV)

Preparing your bulk upload file

To ensure your bulk upload is successful you must:

  • add new venues to the service prior to your bulk upload
  • include existing courses and new courses on the file
  • complete all mandatory fields
  • follow the format requirements
  • save the file as a CSV
