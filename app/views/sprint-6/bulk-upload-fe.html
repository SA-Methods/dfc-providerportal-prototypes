Bulk upload course information

You can add, edit and delete courses on Publish to the course directory individually from the homescreen, or by bulk upload. If you have less than 25 courses to add, edit or delete we would recommend you use the links from the homescreen for these courses.

The maximum number of rows completed in your spreadsheet of courses and locations will determine how your bulk upload is processed.

Creating your file

Uploading and publishing a bulk upload file will replace all of your published courses. To create the file you can:

  • Download a blank template to add your course information to
  • Add, edit or delete a file containing your published course information
