Formatting a bulk upload file

The tables below show information about each field in the bulk upload file and how to complete it correctly.

STANDARD_CODE

Standards are listed by LARS (Learning Aims Reference) or QAN (Qualification Accreditation Number) code.

Codes can be found at Find a learning aim.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
Notes
 Standards must be approved for delivery.

STANDARD_VERSION

Standards codes on Publish to the course directory are version 1.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter 1

FRAMEWORKS_CODE

Frameworks have now been replaced by standards, while we continue to update the service leave this column blank.

Read more about the withdrawal of apprenticeship frameworks.

FRAMEWORKS_PROG_TYPE

Frameworks have now been replaced by standards, while we continue to update the service leave this column blank.

Read more about the withdrawal of apprenticeship frameworks.

FRAMEWORKS_PATHWAY_CODE

Frameworks have now been replaced by standards, while we continue to update the service leave this column blank.

Read more about the withdrawal of apprenticeship frameworks.

APPRENTICESHIP_INFORMATION

Provide information for employers about how you will deliver this apprenticeship training course. The information should meet style and compliance guidelines.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 750
Notes
 Avoid using special characters and line breaks.

APPRENTICESHIP_WEBPAGE

The webpage for this apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory No
Example http://www.apprenticeshipwebpage.com

CONTACT_EMAIL

The contact for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
Example name@example.com

CONTACT_PHONE

The contact for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
Example 01632 960 001

CONTACT_URL

The contact page for the apprenticeship training course.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory No
Example http://college/contactus.com

DELIVERY_METHOD

Where the apprenticeship training will be delivered.

When to complete All delivery methods.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 Classroom
Employer
Both
Notes
 For apprenticeship training courses with more than one VENUE, each apprenticeship must be entered on a separate row.

VENUE

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is classroom or both.
Mandatory Yes
Character limit 255
Notes
 Apprenticeships with the same standard code and the same delivery method must be entered on a separate row with a different venue.

RADIUS

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is both.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter
 xx for example xx
Notes
 A radius must not be more than 874 miles

DELIVERY_MODE

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is classroom or both.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Day;block;both
Notes
 Each unique value can only be listed once (Test what this means)

ACROSS_ENGLAND

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is both
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
No
Notes

NATIONAL_DELIVERY

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is employer based.
Mandatory Yes
What to enter Yes
No

REGION

This field must be kept blank.

SUB_REGION

All the areas where you can deliver the course.

When to complete Where DELIVERY_METHOD is employer based.
Mandatory Yes
Example Derby;Norfolk;Leicester;Brent
What to enter Regions and sub regions
